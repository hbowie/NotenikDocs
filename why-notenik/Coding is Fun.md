Title:  Coding is Fun

Seq:    2.3

Level:  3

Body:

Coding is the practice of typing commands that tell a computer to do something fun and/or useful. 

And coding – whether you're writing COBOL or Swift or HTML or anything else – is fun! 

Fun to learn, fun to explore, and fun to produce visible and meaningful results. 

Coding feels like a superpower, because it's not you that's doing all the work – the computer is doing the heavy lifting – but you're the one tapping into all this power! And figuring out how to harness it, and shape it, and fashion it into something that bears your mark. 

It's tremendously empowering.
